# Nigeria Brewery Sales Performance Dashboard

**OVERVIEW**
This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance for Nigeria Brewery across multiple regions and sales representatives.
This project focuses on total revenue, regional performance, top performing salespeople, and revenue contribution by region to provide actionable business insights.

**OBJECTIVES**
- Measures total company revenue across all sales records
- Identify top-performing sales representatives
- Compare regional sales performance'
- Highlight strongest and weakest sales regions
- Analyze revenue contribution by geography
- Support sales strategy and territory planning.

**KEY INSIGHTS**
- Total recorded revenue reached $786k across all sales entries
- The East region was the highest performing territory with $211,189 in revenue
- The North region generated the lowest total revenue at $165,287 signaling room for growth.
- Mark emerged as the top sales representative with $162,444 in sales.
- Revenue concentratation by region suggests opportunities for targeted expansionand resource allocation.
